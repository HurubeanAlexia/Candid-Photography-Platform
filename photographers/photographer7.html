About me

My style is natural and mixes natural light with beautiful locations and details. Through my photography, I aim to capture true feelings and emotions whilst making you feel at ease with my gentle and chatty approach. I photograph a lot of weddings, as they are popular here on the island due to all the amazing venues. Before becoming a photographer, I had a successful but hectic 10 year career designing and planning urban growth and development in central London. However, I felt like there was something missing and dreamt of leading a more creative life. When I found my way to photography, I did everything in my power to make it happen.
