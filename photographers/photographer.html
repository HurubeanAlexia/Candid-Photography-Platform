About me

I consider myself a visual storyteller, and so I always emphasize the story unfolding around me. The word photography means writing the light and the process of photographing drives me to communicate with people and my surroundings through a constant process of creativity and authentic expression. In my view, photography is not only about freezing moments, but also about revealing the stories hidden in every scene and every expression. I like to explore the world with an artist's eye, always looking for compositions and details that tell a story.
