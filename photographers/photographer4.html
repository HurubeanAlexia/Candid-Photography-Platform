About me

I love love and I´m a hopeless romantic! Most days you can find me drinking a cup of coffee daydreaming about my next photo shoot. I’ve been a photographer for 5 years, and throughout this period I've had the chance and time to explore, feel and experience photography at its best. I've discovered that capturing people and love is what makes my heart tremble. Whether it's a couple in love gazing at each other with sparkling eyes or a family lovingly embracing each other, I find joy in every shot that conveys the warmth and intimacy of love. For me, photography is a way to tell stories of love that will last forever in the hearts of those who look at them.
