About me

I want my photographs to have a timeless feeling, to portray ambience and magic. My style is feminine and full of light, and I pursue smiles and intimate looks when I am shooting. I think photography is all about connection and atmosphere, about freezing the moment and cherishing it forever. I consider myself not only a photographer but a memory-keeper, and I firmly believe memories are our greatest possession.
